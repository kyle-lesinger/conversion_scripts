EARTH OBSERVATION PRODUCTS AND THEIR SUBSETS
=============================================

1. SENTINEL-1 (C-BAND SAR)
---------------------------
- Water Extent
- Flood Proxy Map (FPM)
- RGB Composites
- Coherence
- Amplitude
- Backscatter (VV/VH polarization)
- Interferometric products

2. SENTINEL-2 (MULTISPECTRAL OPTICAL)
--------------------------------------
- True Color
- Natural Color
- Color Infrared (CIR)
- SWIR (Short-Wave Infrared)
- NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index)
- MNDWI (Modified Normalized Difference Water Index)
- Flood Proxy Map (FPM)
- Water Extent

3. LANDSAT SERIES (7/8/9)
--------------------------
- True Color
- Natural Color
- Color Infrared (CIR)
- Panchromatic
- Thermal Infrared (TIR)
- NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index)
- MNDWI (Modified Normalized Difference Water Index)
- dNBR (Differenced Normalized Burn Ratio)

4. MODIS (TERRA/AQUA)
---------------------
- Thermal Anomalies/Fire Detection
- Land Surface Temperature
- True Color Imagery
- Vegetation Indices
- Active Fire Products

5. VIIRS (SUOMI NPP/NOAA-20/NOAA-21)
-------------------------------------
- Black Marble HD (BMHD)
- Day/Night Band (DNB)
- Thermal Infrared
- True Color
- Ash Index
- SO2 (Sulfur Dioxide)
- Aerosol
- Flood Products
- Fire Detection
- Night Lights

6. PLANET LABS
--------------
- True Color
- Color Infrared (CIR)
- Water Extent
- Surface Reflectance

7. MAXAR (WORLDVIEW/GEOEYE)
----------------------------
- Very High Resolution True Color
- Multispectral Analysis
- Panchromatic
- Change Detection Products

8. ARIA (INSAR ANALYSIS)
-------------------------
- Damage Proxy Map (DPM)
- Flood Proxy Map (FPM)
- Coherence Maps
- Displacement Maps
- Ground Deformation
- Interferograms

9. ASTER
--------
- VNIR (15m resolution)
- SWIR (30m resolution)
- TIR (90m Thermal Infrared)
- Digital Elevation Models

10. MASTER (AIRBORNE)
----------------------
- 50-band Hyperspectral
- Thermal Infrared
- Fire Characterization
- Calibration/Validation Data

11. ECOSTRESS (ISS)
--------------------
- Land Surface Temperature (70m)
- Evapotranspiration
- Water Stress Indices
- Thermal Anomalies

12. HLS (HARMONIZED LANDSAT SENTINEL-2)
----------------------------------------
- 30m Harmonized Surface Reflectance
- Combined temporal coverage
- Vegetation Indices
- Water Products (DSWX)

13. SMAP
--------
- Soil Moisture (40km)
- Freeze/Thaw State
- Surface Soil Moisture (~5cm depth)

14. GPM/IMERG
-------------
- Precipitation Rate
- Precipitation Accumulation
- 30-minute Updates
- Rainfall Estimates

15. GOES (GEOSTATIONARY)
-------------------------
- Weather Monitoring
- Fire Detection
- Lightning Mapping
- 5-15 minute Imaging
- Thermal Infrared

16. BURN INDEX PRODUCTS (DERIVED)
----------------------------------
- NBR (Normalized Burn Ratio)
- dNBR (Differenced NBR)
- Burn Severity Classification

17. UAVSAR (NASA JPL AIRBORNE L-BAND SAR)
------------------------------------------
- Pauli RGB Composites
- Polarimetric Data (HH, HV, VV)
- Displacement Maps (InSAR)
- RGB Stacks
- High-resolution Interferometry
- Ground Deformation Monitoring
- Landslide Detection

18. OTHER SPECIALIZED PRODUCTS
-------------------------------

RADARSAT-2:
- C-band SAR imagery
- Water extent mapping
- Flood detection

ALOS-2 (PALSAR-2):
- L-band SAR
- InSAR products
- Amplitude imagery
- Displacement maps

OMPS:
- SO2 (Sulfur Dioxide) measurements
- UV Aerosol Index (UVAI)
- Aerosol products
- Volcanic monitoring

BLACK MARBLE HD:
- High-resolution night lights
- Power outage detection
- Urban monitoring

DFO (DARTMOUTH FLOOD OBSERVATORY):
- Flood extent products
- Multi-sensor composites
- Maximum flood extent

SATELLOGIC:
- High-cadence optical imaging
- Cloud masks
- Surface reflectance

MISR:
- Multi-angle imaging
- Stereo height products
- Cloud detection

TROPICS:
- Microwave soundings
- Precipitation
- Temperature profiles

Note: The following systems are NOT present in the DRCS data:
- EMIT (Earth Surface Mineral Dust Source Investigation)
- ICESat/ICESat-2
- GEDI (Global Ecosystem Dynamics Investigation)
- PeruSat
